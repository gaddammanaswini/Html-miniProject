Digital System and Computer Electronics

Digital electronics is a field of electronics involving the study of digital signals and the engineering of devices that use or produce them. Digital electronic circuits are usually made from large assemblies of logic gates, often packaged in integrated circuits.

M Tech (Digital Systems and Computer Electronics) is a two-year degree program that emphasizes the significance of learning and understanding the basics of digital systems, computer electronics, and their applications in solving emerging and complex problems.

The M.Tech Digital Electronics and Communication System is a full-time course. The course focuses on any form of communication system in telecommunications that uses light as the transmission medium. The subject of this course is analog electronics, signals and systems ,electromagnetic Theory, digital signal processing, analog and digital communications, integrated electronics and communication networks. The applicant’s scope of work includes data communication, broadcasting, research and development, consulting and system support.
