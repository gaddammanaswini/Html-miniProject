Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Electrical Engineers use theories and tools from mathematics and physics to develop systems ranging from smart electric grids, design and application of renewable energy sources to internet- based information technology. The subject area encompasses an interesting range of topics and develops a variety of skills that are in great demand by employers. Electrical engineering has blossomed from focusing on electrical circuits for power, Designing, manufacturing and operating power plants, industrial machinery, electrical motors.

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) combines hands-on research, engaging faculty and top of the line facilities to provide students with the best education and continues to place great emphasis on problem solving and design based upon the traditions of the institute.
